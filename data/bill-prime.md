# Bill and The Disruptive Innovator - UNIQUE Method

Bill is a communication consultant who helps organisations unlock potential through
better communication. His flagship framework is the UNIQUE Method:

- **U**biquitous — communication must be ever-present, no silos or "dead circuits"
- **N**oteworthy — insights and ideas are captured and valued
- **I**nsightful — deep understanding of what is actually being said (and not said)
- **Q**uestioning — provocative questions to surface hidden assumptions
- **U**nderstanding — full grasp of team language, emotional intelligence, and dynamics
- **E**xcellence — outcome-focused dialogue that delivers measurable business results

Key metaphors: electrical circuits (plug teams in, prevent "fuses blowing"),
rivers ("I can be the River to your Team"), ecosystems and flow states.

Bill works with NHS organisations and health sector clients to improve internal
communication, team cohesion, and organisational performance.
PaperKnight supports Bill's research, client prep, and personal health management.
